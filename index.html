Ambatueat is a restaurant, bar and coffee roastery located on a busy corner site in Farringdon’s Exmouth Market. With glazed frontage on two sides of the building, overlooking the market and a bustling London intersec.

Ambatueat is the place for a fine dining experience where the tradition is blended with an imaginative and modern twist.
